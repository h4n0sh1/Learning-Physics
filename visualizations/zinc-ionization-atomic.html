Energy barrier for electron loss
Barrier lowered by circuit & OH⁻
Step 1: Zn atom at surface, 4s electrons are loosely held.
Step 2: OH⁻ ions approach, ready to stabilize Zn²⁺.
Step 3: External circuit "pulls" electrons away, lowering energy barrier.
Step 4: Zn atom loses 2 electrons, becomes Zn²⁺.
Step 5: Zn²⁺ binds with 2OH⁻ to form Zn(OH)₂, removing Zn²⁺ from solution and driving more electron loss.
